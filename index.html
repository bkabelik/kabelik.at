Kabelik GmbH is a specialized provider of high-end geospatial services and complex IT infrastructure solutions. Based in Wiener Neustadt, we operate at the intersection of aerial data acquisition and advanced data processing.

Our focus lies on the entire value chain of remote sensing: from the strategic planning and execution of sensor missions to the high-performance processing and refinement of massive datasets into actionable Business Intelligence. We support industrial partners and utility providers in managing complex spatial data—efficiently, scalably, and at the highest technological level.
